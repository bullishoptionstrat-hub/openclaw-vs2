==============================================================================
EXECUTION MODES
==============================================================================

PAPER_VALIDATION (Default):
- All signals processed as paper trades
- No broker connection
- Safety controls enforced
- Perfect for validation phase
- No capital at risk

PAPER_LIVE:
- Paper trades run concurrently with live
- Comparison view: paper vs live execution
- Used for confidence building before deployment
- Still no capital at risk

LIVE_TRADING:
- Signals routed to broker for real execution
- Only after validation gate PASS
- Real capital deployed
- Must explicitly switch mode + confirm

Mode Switch Safety:
- Explicit method call required (can't happen by accident)
- Safety confirmation logging
- Validates gate passes before switch allowed

DAILY METRICS COLLECTED

Per Trading Day:
